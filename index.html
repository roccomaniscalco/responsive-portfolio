About Me

My name is Rocco Maniscalco. I am an eighteen-year-old full-time student at Georgia State University majoring in Computer Science. I am currently enrolled as a part-time student in the Georgia Tech coding boot camp program with a focus on fullstack web development. I graduated from Walton High School in 2020. In highschool, I completed two computer science courses with a focus on object oriented Java programming and served as vice president for the Walton chapter of Coding for Community. My goal is to integrate beautiful design with beautiful code to create lasting virtual experiences. In my free time I love to explore nature, make electronic music, and study political science.
